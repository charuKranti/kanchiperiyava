Anna Dhaana Kainkaryam

We are pleading on behalf of thousands and lakhs of people who are starving for food and struggling for their daily needs. We are so blessed in this birth, inspite of whatever sins we have on our back, still bagavan has blessed us with food and shelter.

Why are we special? While we are having a comfort life, why are others struggling in front of us. To make it precise, Maha Periyava says whatever dharma ( the offerings) we made is protecting us now.

“अन्नधानं समं धानम् त्रिलोकेषु न विधते” Anna dhaanam samam dhaanam trilokeshu na vidhathe.


These verses are from VEDA:

It says ANNADHAANAM IS THE MOST SUPREME, INCOMPARABLE, THE MOST PIOUS AND MERITORIOUS OF ALL CHARITY.

Thus according to Vedas, the one who does annadanam to the poor, attains heaven (Punya Loka) in this Universe (Brahmanda) itself, which means you get all your wishes fulfilled in this world (samsara) while we live , and also attain heavenly abode after life. In this whole universe, the creation and its progression depends on food.

Hence giving food to the poor and the needy is more than attaining heaven. It is only when one is given food, however much that person may take; there is a limit beyond which he just cannot take. The person says 'Thrupthosmi' (meaning ‘enough I am satisfied’). When food which is offered exceeds the limits, the person begs 'Please do not serve any more'. when full satisfaction is expressed like this; the giver also reaps the full reward of charity

“Gaja turaga Sahasram Gokulam koti danam
Kanaka Rajatha patram Methini sagarantham
Upaya kula vishuttam Koti kanya pradanam
Nahi nahi bahu danam Annadanam samanam”

Donating 1000 elephants & horses, Donating 10 million cows, Donating any number of vessels of silver and gold, Donating the entire land till sea, Offering the entire services of the clan, Helping in the marriage of 10 million women, none of the above can ever be equal to ANNADHANAM, the feeding of hungry and the needy.

Is it not so easy & simple to do this sath karma ( good deed) and attain punya.

As Maha upanishad says "VASUDHAIVA KUTUMBAKAM" , whole world is our family , let us spare a small / meager amount of our salary everymonth towards this good cause.
