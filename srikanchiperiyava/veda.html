Veda Rakshanam

Veda Rakshanam

“वेदोऽखिलो धर्ममूलं स्मृतिशीले च तद्विदाम् ।
आचारश्चैव साधूनामात्मनस्तुष्टिरेव च ॥ ६ ॥”

vedo'khilo dharmamūlaṃ smṛtiśīle ca tadvidām |
ācāraścaiva sādhūnāmātmanastuṣṭireva ca || 6 ||

The entire Veda is the root-source of Dharma; also the Conscientious Recollection of righteous persons versed in the Veda, the Practice of Good (and learned) Men, and their self-satisfaction.

Money will not come with us or our children when we leave this world. Only the Good Deeds we do will come with us says - SHRI MAHAPERIYAVA.

We may all have hailed from different traditions and culture but come under one roof called SANATHANA DHARMA, which connotes the way of life for all humanity. It is based on the VEDIC SCRIPTURE which acts as a foundation, furnish guidelines on all subjects necessary for the well being of humanity and is applicable at all times. It reveals that the paramathma is common to all of us and leading the life as prescribed in the Vedas , will help us realise paramathma in us. We become god ourselves. It is not only sanatana dharma , even other religions that exist all over the world may be traced back to the VEDA.

They are ANAADHI (timeless, without a beginning and APAOURSHAM (not emanating from any Individual living being). They are deemed to be the life-breath of Bhagavan Sri Maha Vishnu. "Yasya Niswasitham Vedaha". And for Sri Maha Periyava who is sakshath parameshwara swaroopam , it was/is/will be “Veda Rakshanam”.

The numbers of those dedicating themselves to the study of Vedas had diminished and declining due to an uncertain future, with no promise of even a minimum economic

livelihood. His holiness Shri Maha Periyava was greatly worried over the decline of the traditional Vedic education resulting in the scarcity of Vedic scholars carrying our great Vedic heritage to the next generation. Thus, there was a fear that this great treasure, beneficial to all mankind, being lost forever. Only because of Shri Maha Periyava's sincere effort and blessings we have this many patashalas existing now.

Its our primary duty to nourish and cherish the Vedas , Isnt?As per Hindu Culture, whoever does the veda adhyayanam is equivalent to Lord Shiva. Veda shiva: shivoveda: vedaadyaayi sadaashiva: So whatever we offer to vedic scholars and vedic students are accepted directly by lord parameshwara himself says Shri Periyava. Let us not commit the unforgivable sin by breaking the chain of Vedic study, which was brought back into existence through extensive, enormous and immeasurable effort of shri periyava through many patashalas across India.

We at KANCHI PERIYAVA DHARMA RAKSHANA TRUST aims at supporting the vedic study for the peace and welfare of the entire world by providing all basic requirements of Paatashaalas students like Groceries,Books, Palakai ( aasanam) , Chikku Mounchi ( book holder) , Palasa thandam, Vratti (cow dung cakes), Samithu ( peepal tree sticks) , HomaKundam, damani, ( brass blower) Koormasanam, Vastram etc under the direction of Shri Shri Shri Bala Periyava who is the disciple and swaroopam of shri Maha periyava himself.

We also provide rudraksham Swarnabhandhanam to the Vedic students as per the need.

  • Annadhanam (grocery, milk, and vegetables)
  • Provide nitya homam dhraviam such as vratties, samith etc.
  • Conduct and support veda parayanam
  • Help agnihotra communities

Vedic Chanting, according to the prescribed rules, leads not only to collective prosperity and individual well being, but also avoids natural calamities and mitigates evils . Invocating Devatas and their blessings, leads to universal prosperity, ushering peace and contentment everywhere.

Last but not the least-

Deivaththin Kural being yet another Bhagavat Gita blessed by Sri Maha Periyava has emphasized on all of us the importance of performing our Nithya Karma Anushtaanams regularly or at least our Sandhyavandanam of which Gayatri Japam is a big part.

Na gaayathryaa: paro mantra:

Na maathu: daivatham param

There is no mantra superior than a Gayathri manthra
There is no deity more than our Mother
Says this sloka

Shri Mahaperiyava almost pleads with us to perform 1008 Gayatri everyday, if not at least 108, or 54 or 12 being the barest minimum. Regardless of veda samrakshanam or not, let us do our nithya karma without fail.

The purpose of this trust is to encourage everyone to follow their anushtaanamas and take a small part for veda kainkaryam consistently and promote veda rakshanam which has been the life time mission of Shri Maha Periyava ..

Let us give a serious thought to this call of our GURUNATHA.

Jaya Jaya Shankara Hara Hara Shankara
